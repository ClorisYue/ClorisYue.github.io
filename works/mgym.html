MGYM

Time: Aug. 2017 - Dec. 2017

Personal project for SI582 - Introduction of Interaction Design

Design Processes:

Background Research & User Interview -> Ideation -> Brainstroming & Sketches -> User Analysis & Persona -> Stroy Mapping & Empathy Map -> Paper Prototype -> User Testing -> High-fidelity Prototype -> Iteration

00. The Background

Many people mentioned the problem that when they arrived at the gyms the facilities they wanted are taken up by others, so they had to wait or change their plans.
Also, the UM gyms provide a lot of high quality courses, but not many people noticed these information. For those who are new to the gyms, they lack information about the gyms and the training courses/guidance provided.

01. The Goal of MGym

- Provide real-time occupancy information of facilities in the gyms or sport fields.
- Allow users to make appointments of facilities in advance.
- Provide training events’ information to exercisers, and especially, people who are new to the gyms. 

02.Target Users

Primary: Regular Exercisers

- Usually do not need guidance
- High frequency
- Familiar with various equipment and use them frequently

Secondary: Recreational Users

- Sometimes need training guidance
- Low frequency
- Want to try some exercise with guidance
