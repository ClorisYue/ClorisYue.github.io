MicroDoc

Description:

Students has to get up early to register in line in the fifth affiliated hospital SUN YAT-SEN UNIVERSITY to which is half an hour’s drive from the SYSU Zhuhai campus. What’s more, in order to get the reimbursement of medical insurance students have to handle referral in campus clinic before they want to treat in hospital off campus. And the application in person for reimbursement is so complex.
Based on above problems, we came up the idea of online health management system which mainly has following functions:
Online consultation
Registration
Referral registration
Medical insurance reimbursement application

MicroDoc which developed in February, 2016 aim to provide a convenient health management service. It is course project of Mobile Web Development and Database Development, finished by my 4 people team.
