SMIE News Center

SMIE News Center was established in October, 2014. It is one of the organizations which directly affiliated to the faculty. It represents the faculty, in the meanwhile, strengthen students' understanding of their development with faculty's construction and provides a better channel of information delivery.



Logo 09.2014

The logo of SMIE News Center is composed of the English letter "n" and "c", which is the abbreviation of News Center, and forms the pattern of earth. The succint logo deliveries the idea that SMIE News Center informs students of not only the news of faculty but also the latest trends of the industry.



Uniform 09.2014

The central pattern of News Center's uniform is the transformation of "MIE", in the meanwhile, the pictures of pen and camera were embedded into the pattern on the back.

Souvenir for SYSU's 90th anniversary 11.2014
